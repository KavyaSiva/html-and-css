Map

It takes 51 minutes to travel from Bangalore Electronic City to Wonderla Bangalore. Approximate driving distance between Bangalore Electronic City and Wonderla Bangalore is 43 kms or 26.7 miles or 23.2 nautical miles . Travel time refers to the time taken if the distance is covered by a car.

  • Get on NH48 in Konappana Agrahara, Doddathoguru from NH44 8 min (3.8 km)
  • Follow NH48 to NH275. Exit from NH48 22 min (23.6 km)
  • Continue on NH275 to your destination 17 min (11.6 km)
